Product Information

  • Insightful analytics for identifying key influencers
  • Collaborative platforms for academic discussion

How It Works

Our platform leverages data-driven techniques to map out the connections within your institution. By analyzing publication data, project collaborations, and other relevant metrics, U-Net'It can help you understand and optimize the flow of knowledge among your faculty and staff.
