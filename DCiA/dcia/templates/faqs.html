FAQs

What is network analysis?

Network analysis is the mathematical analysis of complex working procedures in terms of a network of related activities.

What is centrality?

Centrality measures help identify the most important vertices within a graph. For example, individuals with high levels of centrality within a social network might be those with the most influence or connections within the network.

What is an edge/node attribute?

Edge attributes refer to the properties associated with the relationships between nodes, while node attributes refer to the properties of the individual nodes within the network.
